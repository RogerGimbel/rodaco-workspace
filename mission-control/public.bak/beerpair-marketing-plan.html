Response Template 3: "Beer pairing for spicy food?"

Spicy food + beer is tricky. Most people get it wrong. **Don't:** - High-ABV beers (alcohol amplifies heat) - Bitter IPAs (bitterness + capsaicin = harsh) **Do:** - **Wheat beers** (Hefeweizen, Witbier) - Slight sweetness tames spice, carbonation cools - **Lagers** (Mexican, Pilsner) - Clean, crisp, refreshing - **Cream Ales** - Low bitterness, smooth, cooling **Science:** Capsaicin (spice compound) binds to receptors. Carbonation + slight sweetness help neutralize it. Bitterness makes it worse. **Specific examples:** - Spicy Thai ‚Üí Hefeweizen - Buffalo wings ‚Üí Cream Ale - Hot curry ‚Üí K√∂lsch - Nashville hot chicken ‚Üí Pilsner If you want to nerd out on this, I built BeerPair (free app) that analyzes spice level and suggests ideal pairings.
