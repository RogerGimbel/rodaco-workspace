## The 3 Pairing Strategies

1. **COMPLEMENT** - Match similar flavors (chocolate stout + chocolate cake)
2. **CONTRAST** - Opposing flavors create balance (IPA + fried chicken)
3. **BALANCE** - Sweet spot between both (amber ale + burger)

## Carbonation Science
- Carbonation = palate cleanser (scrubs tongue between bites)
- Fat coats tongue, carbonation breaks through
- Match carbonation to food richness:
  - Light meal ‚Üí Low carbonation
  - Medium meal ‚Üí Medium carbonation
  - Heavy meal ‚Üí High carbonation
